HORCRUXES

TOM RIDDLE'S DIARYTom Riddle's diary is a blank diary which he transformed it into a horcrux. Destroyed by Harry .
MARVOLO GAUNT'S RING It is a gold ring inset with a black stone (actually the Resurrection Stone, but neither Marvolo Gaunt nor Lord Voldemort were aware of its existence.
Nagini-The snakeLord Voldemort's pet snake and loyal servant. It did the bidding of her master. Killed by Neville Longbottom with the Sword of Gryffindor.
ROWENA RAVENCLAW'S DIADEMA tiara said to grant wisdom to the wearer. It was lost for centuries before it became a Horcrux. Spent centuries hidden in a forest in Albania until it was moved to the Room of Requirement. Rowena Ravenclaw owned it first .Stolen by Rowena's daughter Helena before it was made into a Horcrux by Lord Voldemort. Destroyed by Fiendfyre.
HELGA HUPPLEPUFF'S CUPSaid to posses several powers, yet these were not thoroughly tested. Hidden in the Gringotts vault of Bellatrix Lestrange .Helga Hufflepuff first owned it. Stolen and made a Horcrux by Lord Voldemort. Destroyed by Hermione Granger.
SALAZAR SLYTHERIN'S LOCKETBrings out the negative emotions of the wearer. Kept in a remote cave until it was replaced with a decoy. Salazar Slytherin first owned. Passed through generations of Gaunts before being pawned by Merope, after which it changed hands many times. Destroyed by Ron Weasley.
HARRY POTTERHe himself realizes about he being a horcrux in the 7th book (HARRY POTTER AND THE DEATHLY HALLOWS) IN Dumbeldore's office after seeing Severus Snape's truth. He is killed by Voldemort to end up being his horcrux and to be successful in killing him.
IMPORTANT NOTE: QUIRININUS QUIRRELL CAN ALSO BE CONSIDERED AS A HALF-HORCRUX AS HE WAS ACTUALLY A TEMPORARY HORCRUX , PROVIDING HIS BODY AS A SUPPORTING BODY TO VOLDEMORT .
